About Our Neighborhood Association

Connecting residents, promoting community initiatives, and making Rosedale a better place to live.

Who We Are

The Frank Rushton Neighborhood Association is a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to fostering a strong, connected, and vibrant community in Rosedale. We bring residents together, provide resources, and advocate for neighborhood improvements.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To strengthen community ties, promote civic engagement, and improve the quality of life for all Rosedale residents.

Vision: A safe, inclusive, and thriving neighborhood where residents are engaged, informed, and empowered.
